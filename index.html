About

I specialize in modeling, analysis, and optimization of pump systems. My graduate research focused on a lumped-parameter model of a dual-lip balanced vane pump and a workflow that ties geometry generation, Multics simulation, and post-processing together for rapid design iteration.

Beyond research, I care about robust engineering processes: unit testing, data hygiene, reproducible runs, and clear documentation that make results trustworthy and transferable.
