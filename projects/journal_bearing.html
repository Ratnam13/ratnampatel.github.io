Journal Bearing Tribology Study

Focus: Hydrodynamic lubrication behavior to support reliability in rotating machinery.

Tools: FreeFEM++, MATLAB (post-processing), ANSYS (supporting analyses)

What I did

  • Analyzed film thickness and pressure distribution trends under varying loads and speeds.
  • Explored wear/contact stress implications and the sensitivity of clearance/tolerances.
  • Compared predicted trends against classical references; summarized design guard-bands.

Outcome

Produced a concise set of design guidelines to mitigate scuffing and minimize wear by balancing clearance, load, and lubricant behavior. Results informed reliability considerations for pump components in other projects.
