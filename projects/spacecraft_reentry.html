Spacecraft Re-entry Thermal–Structural Study

Goal: Evaluate heating loads and insulation trade-offs for re-entry conditions; assess structural response and margins.

Tools: ANSYS (thermal/structural), OpenFOAM/ANSYS Fluent (heating estimates), MATLAB (post-processing)

Scope

  • Estimated external heat flux profiles and conducted transient through-thickness thermal analyses for candidate TPS stacks.
  • Assessed temperature limits at structure interfaces; evaluated mass vs. peak temperature trade-offs.
  • Ran structural checks with thermal loads to confirm margin under representative events.

Outcome

Delivered a set of recommended stack-ups and thickness ranges that meet temperature limits with reasonable mass, plus guidance for further test/qualification.
