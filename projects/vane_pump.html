Hydraulic Vane Pump Optimization

Tools: CATIA V5, ANSYS Mechanical/Fluent, FreeFEM++, MATLAB

In my graduate research at Purdue’s Maha Fluid Power Research Center, I designed and optimized dual-lip vane pump components under high-pressure operating conditions. Using DOE-driven optimization, I achieved up to 25.7% reduction in flow ripple and 8% improvement in volumetric efficiency compared to the industry reference cam-ring profile.

I also developed and operated hydraulic test rigs with instrumentation and data acquisition, validating simulation predictions with experimental data. This gave me hands-on exposure to both failure diagnostics and manufacturability assessments.
